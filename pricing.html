Frequently asked questions.

Common details about billing, transcription hours, exports, and team usage.

What happens when I run out of transcription hours?

You can upgrade your plan or add pay-as-you-go hours without changing your existing workspace.

Do unused hours roll over?

Plan hours reset each billing cycle. Pay-as-you-go hours stay available until they are used.

Can I export subtitles on the free plan?

Yes. Starter supports SRT, VTT, and TXT export for projects within the free 30 minute allowance.

Is burn-in included?

Burn-in subtitle rendering is included with Pro and Business plans, along with reusable caption templates.

Does Business include API access?

Yes. Business includes API access for automated upload, transcription, export, and status workflows.

Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

Yes. You can switch billing periods from workspace settings before the next renewal.

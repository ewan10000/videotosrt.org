Frequently asked questions.

Details teams usually need before moving subtitle work into a dedicated editor.

Which file types can I upload?

VideoToSRT supports common video and audio formats including MP4, MOV, WebM, MP3, WAV, and M4A.

Can I edit captions after generation?

Yes. The editor supports transcript edits, line splitting, merging, timecode adjustment, and preview playback.

Does it only export SRT?

No. You can export SRT, VTT, TXT, and CSV depending on your publishing or review workflow.

Can teams review subtitles together?

Business workspaces include shared projects, reviewer access, and role-based export permissions.
